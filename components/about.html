About Me

Brian Jay Eleazar

I am an IT student at STI College with 2 years of experience in portrait photography. My passion lies in capturing the unique stories and personalities of individuals through my lens.

My approach to portrait photography is deeply personal. I believe that every face tells a unique story, and my mission is to create an environment where your authentic self can shine through. Whether in my studio or on location, I work to establish a connection that allows for genuine expression.

When I'm not behind the camera, I'm constantly studying the art of portraiture, from classical paintings to contemporary works, seeking inspiration to bring fresh perspectives to my work.
